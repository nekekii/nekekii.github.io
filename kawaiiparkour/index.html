About

KawaiiParkour is a Spigot Minecraft server plugin to have your players time their parkour runs!

Current Released For: 1.16.3 â€” Latest version: v1.0

Features

  • Easy parkour setup.
  • Multiple courses.
  • Personal bests and course records.
  • Configurable cosmetic options.
  • Permissions support.

Commands

  • All Permissions
    • /parkour checkpoint - Puts you to your last checkpoint.
    • /parkour reset - Puts you to the beginning of the parkour.
    • /parkour info <name> - Shows info about a course.
    • /parkour help - Shows list of commands.

  • KawaiiParkour.list
    • /parkour list - Lists all courses.

  • KawaiiParkour.create
    • /parkour create <name> - Sets up a new parkour course with given name.
    • /parkour cancel - Cancels parkour creation process.

  • KawaiiParkour.delete
    • /parkour delete <name> - Deletes a parkour with given name.

  • KawaiiParkour.cleartimes
    • /parkour cleartimes <name> - Clears all times for given course.
    • /parkour cleartoptime <name> - Clears top time for given course.

  • KawaiiParkour.option
    • /parkour option <option> - Toggles given option.
Permission KawaiiParkour.admin gives all these permissions!

FAQ

How do I prevent my players from taking fall damage?

I'd reccomend using something like WorldGuard or gamerules to prevent that.

How do I create a parkour?

Place a iron pressure plate for your start point, a stone pressure plate for any checkpoints, and a gold pressure plate for the end point of your course. Then use /parkour create <name> and step on the pressure plates in order. Once you get to the gold pressure plate, the parkour will be enabled!

How do I stop/start the fireworks/meows from happening?

Use /parkour option <option> or change it in the config and restart the server.
